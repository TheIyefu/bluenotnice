BLUE NOTNICE?

BlueNotNice, a portrait photographer rooted in the lively atmosphere of Abuja, Nigeria, captures the essence of unique personalities through his lens. With an adventure that began in 2019, partly to impress a girl with his newfound photography skills, BlueNotNice found his true calling. The vibrant colors and diverse characters of Abuja fuel his passion, as he transforms each portrait into a story.
‍
Since moving to Abuja in 2015, BlueNotNice has immersed himself in the city's rhythm, using his camera to explore the depths of individuality and style. His creative process is a dance of collaboration, getting to know each subject intimately, ensuring their inner light shines in every shot.

From a quirky start to being featured in DeedsMagazine, BlueNotNice's journey is a testament to his dedication and artistic evolution. Dreaming big, he aspires to blend the bold creations of a renowned fashion designer with his photography, crafting a masterpiece of visual symphony.

In essence, BlueNotNice isn't just a photographer; he's a storyteller, a dreamer, and an artist painting the canvas of human connection with every click of his shutter.
